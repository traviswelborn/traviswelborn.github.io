Mission, Goals and Education

The Arlington Humane Society is a privately funded society founded for helping animals through awareness and education, including obedience training, vaccinations, lost and found, adoptions and many more.

Adopt Your New Best Friend

The Arlington Humane Society serves to promote owner responsibility. Locals have the option of adopting from the Society. Adoptions cost $75 (non-refundable) and include all vaccinations, spaying/neutering and obedience training prior to adoption. Contact us for more information.
